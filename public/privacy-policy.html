1. Introduction

Welcome to PlayListify ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights.

2. Data We Collect

When you use PlayListify, we collect and process the following data:

  • Authentication data through Google and Spotify
  • Your email address (provided through OAuth)
  • Profile information from connected services
  • Playlist data from Spotify
  • Usage data and preferences

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide our playlist conversion service
  • To authenticate you with Spotify and YouTube
  • To maintain and improve our service
  • To communicate with you about our service

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Spotify API for accessing your playlists
  • YouTube API for creating playlists
  • Google OAuth for authentication

5. Data Storage

We do not permanently store your playlists or music data. This data is only processed temporarily during the conversion process.

7. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
